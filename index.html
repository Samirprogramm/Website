Is Ducky V1 legal to use?

Ducky V1 is a research and education tool. Its use must comply with all applicable laws in your jurisdiction. Only use it on devices and networks you own or have explicit authorization to test. Unauthorized interception of signals is illegal.

What firmware does it run?

Ducky V1 ships with custom open firmware based on the ESP-IDF framework. You can flash custom firmware via USB-C. Community-maintained alternative firmwares are also available.

Can I extend the functionality?

Absolutely. The open firmware and GPIO breakout pins allow you to add sensors, modules, and custom hardware. The community actively develops plugins and add-ons.

What's in the box?

Ducky V1 unit, USB-C cable, external antenna (SMA), quick-start guide, and sticker pack. Protective case available as an add-on.
